Skywind

2015-05-26

Skywind an EDM trance track took around 40 hours to produce. I spent a lot of time experimenting with getting various percussive and synth dynamics right.

Each of the “Edit” variants is mostly the same, with a few tweaks. These versions can never be re-created again due to loss of source material, so I uploaded them.
