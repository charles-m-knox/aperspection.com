Reflex

2014-11-01

Reflex is my personal favorite. It is a more abstract style of music that doesn’t really follow most patterns, and is inspired by Flume and Jon Hopkins.

The Extended Edit is actually an earlier version of the track that is not mixed as well, but contains a more cinematic introduction.
