Space Codes

2021-03-30

Space Codes consists of 3 60-minute tracks that are meant to help you focus.

Each song in the release follows a similar repetitive pattern with variance over the span of each hour-long listen. The songs are meant to become background noise, with the intention of helping you reach higher productivity every time you need to sit down and focus.

I have a few songs that I often listen to on Spotify that are long:

  • Cosmoscope by au5
  • Solar One by The Flashbulb

I’ve found that these songs really help me get into a specific mindset, especially for focusing. I wanted to take this and give it back to the world with some of my own long-play songs.
