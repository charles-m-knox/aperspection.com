Ascending

2013-09-08

Ascending consists of two tracks that both have a climactic build-up over the duration of the song.

The Spooky Climax Edit is basically the same song, except for the final climax, and it is a bit of a joke - I was having some fun with dubstep and it ended up sounding like it belonged in some sort of halloween-styled track.
