Sessel

2021-02-23

Sessel is a song takes a lot of inspiration from uplifting trance.. The song’s strength is in the foundation - the moving piano strokes with the simple, yet strong chord progression allows a nice, smooth flow into a relaxed break in the middle of the song, followed by a build-up into a more elated and aggressive peak at the end.
