She Sings Colors

2013-05-10

She Sings Colors consists of two tracks. I created the original track as a draft, and a friend heard it and wanted to collaborate on it. We sent a few versions back and forth, but did not land on a finished result. The DSE edit is the last draft.

As for the song itself, it is inspired by drum and bass but doesn’t follow the typical genre. It features more powerful EDM-like saw synths with an uplifting chord progression. I found it difficult to bring the song to fruition amidst my college studies, and still consider it largely unfinished to date.
