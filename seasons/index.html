Seasons

2021-02-23

Seasons is an aggressive, 140-bpm, uplifting, melodic trance tune that takes listeners on a journey through the four seasons, starting from fall and ending in summer.

Trained music producers will note the challenges faced with the audio compression and mixing. This song came about from some extra creativity that occurred while between classes in college - I had just purchased a copy of Sylenth1 and had to experiment with it.
