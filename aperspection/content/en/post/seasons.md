Seasons is an aggressive, 140-bpm, uplifting, melodic trance tune that takes listeners on a journey through the four seasons: the rhythm of fall, the crystalline winter, the melting away of winter into spring, and the joy and happiness of summer, all in order.

Inspired and influenced by the rhythms and melodies of *Heatbeat*, *BT*, and *Arty*, Seasons pushes the boundaries of what it means to hear a dense, illustrious soundscape, with finely crafted synths and piano key strokes that redefine each phrase of the song.

Trained music producers will note the challenges faced with the audio compression and mixing. This song was an explosion of creativity that occurred while between classes in college - I had just purchased a copy of Sylenth1 and had to make it shine.

Enjoy Seasons.
